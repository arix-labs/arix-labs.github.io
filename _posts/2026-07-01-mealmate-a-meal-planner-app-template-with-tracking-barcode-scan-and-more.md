# **How Does a White Label Nutrition App Work?**
A white label nutrition app is built so the same underlying app can be rebranded and customized for a different niche, coach, or market without rewriting the core tracking logic each time. For a nutrition app, that means calorie tracking, macro calculations, and food logging stay constant, while branding, diet-specific defaults, and recipe content change per launch.
MealMate is structured this way, with onboarding fields like diet preference and allergies feeding into the same underlying tracking system regardless of which niche the app is branded for. This is what makes a genuine white label nutrition app different from a single-purpose app that only allows surface customization — the same core product can support a general fitness audience, a specific diet community, or a coach's client base.
